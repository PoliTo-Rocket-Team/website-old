The Team

At the beginning of the 2022-2023 Academic Year, the Team is composed of more than 70 between undergraduate students, graduate students, and PhD candidates. Most of the Team members come from the aerospace, mechanical, electronic and computer engineering courses of Politecnico di Torino.

Executive

    During his presidency, Fabio expanded the Team from 6 to 75+ members, involving undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral students. Besides directing the Team, he manages the relations with academia, private partners, and other institutions.
