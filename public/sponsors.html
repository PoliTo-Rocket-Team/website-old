Main partners


Politecnico di Torino

Politecnico di Torino (Polytechnic University of Turin) is the oldest public technical university of Italy, located in Turin. Politecnico is consistently ranked as one of the best universities in Italy and in Europe. As of 2022 it is ranked by QS World University Rankings 33rd among the engineering and technology universities in the world. Politecnico's Department of Aerospace and Mechanical Engineering (DIMEAS) is the main partner of the Team.


Mul2 Research Group

Mul2 Research Group is a research project within Politecnico di Torino's Dept. of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, devoted to the development of advanced structural models for MULSs with particular attention given to the multifield analysis and the fluid-structure interactions.
