FAQs

Can I join as a first year student?

Yes, and we encourage you to! Theoretical knowledge and previous experience are appreciated, but they are not everything. Young and motivated students can learn and acquire real experience while working on our projects.

Are there any requirements for joining the team?

Only one: you must have time to dedicate to our projects. We accept all PoliTo students regardless of program and year (undegraduate, graduate and PhD). Students are not required to have any prior experience or knowledge.

Can I join the team as an international student?

Of course! As of the summer of 2022, around 10% of the Team's members are international students. The selection process is the same for international and Italian students. As for any other student, a good English knowledge is preferred.

Do you recruit only aerospace eng. students?

Of course not! Even if the majority of the Team's members come from the aerospace eng. program, some of the Team's positions require knowledge that can be acquired in the electronic, mechanical, automotive, biomedical, and many other programs. Even if you are not enrolled in an engineering program you can still contribute to the Team's projects!

How can I join the Team?

At the beginning of Fall semester we will publish on our Social pages and on our website all the info regarding the application process. An Info Session is planned for the beginning of the semester to present the Team's projects and answer student's questions. You can expect that, in the application process, we will need your CV or resume, an optional motivational letter, and some info about you.

Do you recruit at the beginning of the 2nd semester?

We do not know yet, but it is unlikely. Training new members takes time, and if students join in the winter semester they will start working towards the end of the academic year, which is not ideal.

Students could be recruited at the beginning of the 2nd semester if the possess a particular set of skills or experience which matches the Team's needs.

How much time do I need to put into the team every week?

There is not a rule for how much time you need to put in. The Team is young and does not have a large number of members, so it is expected that the members of the Team put a considerable amount of work for our projects.

In general, we expect that students can provide at the very least 5 hours of work for the Team per week. Exceptions can be made for particular positions, such as those which require particular skills or prior experience.

Can I join the Team if I am not a Politecnico di Torino student, or if I am a high school student?

PoliTo Rocket Team is a student team which gets its resources from the Dept. of Mechanical and Aerospace engineering of Politecnico di Torino. For this reason we can't accept students outside Politecnico. However, is it possible to cooperate and work together on some particular projects. If are working on a rocketry-related project and you want to collaborate with us, contact us.
